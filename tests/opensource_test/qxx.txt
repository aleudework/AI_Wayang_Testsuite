USER_REQUEST: Find the five suppliers who generated the highest total revenue. Revenue is defined as l_extendedprice * (1 - l_discount) aggregated over all lineitems for each supplier. Return the supplier information together with the computed revenue. Output in textfile.
SQL_QUERY:
SELECT
    s.s_suppkey,
    s.s_name,
    s.s_address,
    s.s_phone,
    SUM(l.l_extendedprice * (1 - l.l_discount)) AS total_revenue
FROM
    supplier s
    JOIN lineitem l ON s.s_suppkey = l.l_suppkey
GROUP BY
    s.s_suppkey,
    s.s_name,
    s.s_address,
    s.s_phone
ORDER BY
    total_revenue DESC
LIMIT 5;
