USER_REQUEST: Make a plan that return organisation names and the number of departments that they have. I want the output in descending order and outputted in a textfile. 
SQL_QUERY:
SELECT 
    o.name,
    COUNT(d.department_id) AS departments
FROM organisation o
LEFT JOIN department d 
    ON d.organisation_id = o.organisation_id
GROUP BY o.organisation_id, o.name
ORDER BY departments DESC;
